Junior Developer Project 2

Reflection

For me the most challenging part of this project was trying to work with people that didn't communiucate. For me there were almost no challenges other than trying to communicate with either a Lead Developer that never added me, to a client that never responded to questions. Even then these things weren't too large of an issue for me. I enjoyed getting to work on another persons idea. My ideas differ from those around me so it was fun to see what others wanted. If I were to do this project again, I would have probably decided to do everything in one day and then ask the clients afterwards if there were any changes they would have liked, as this would have made it easier to get feedback. I feel confident in my skills to do things of this sort in the real world, as I feel I was fairly organized and finished most things in a timely manner.
